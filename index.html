More than a marketer 💗

The short version: I love making things people actually want to engage with — whether that's a brand's audience, a Twitch chat, or a kid learning a new skill.

I'm currently the Digital Marketing Manager at EZ Esports, an NYC nonprofit run by Edison Zhong that gives high schoolers real experience competing in the esports scene. I run content strategy, brand identity, and analytics for the org — basically I get to be data nerd and creative director at the same time, which is my favorite combo.

I love picking up new skills and getting a feel for industries I haven't touched yet — before EZ Esports, that curiosity took me across beauty (Rei Cosmetics), wellness (Exuding Wellness), kids' products (Delta Children), and small business marketing (The KOVA), so I've pitched, filmed, edited, and analyzed my way through a lot of different audiences.

Outside of the 9-to-5, I stream League of Legends and VALORANT on Twitch as "KYUXTII," and I spent two years managing a 14-player collegiate League of Legends program (we took 1st place 🏆). I also worked part-time with kids with developmental disabilities — it kept me grounded and honestly made me a better communicator everywhere else.
